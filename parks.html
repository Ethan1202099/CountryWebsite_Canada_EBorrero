Amusment Parks

Galaxy Land, it originally opened on June 27, 1983 and was first called FantasyLand but later changed its name to Galaxy Land. It's known as one of the best Amusment Park's in Canada. It is located inside the West Edmonton Mall

Splash Works, (Also known as Canada's Wonderland) It originally opened in 1992 in Ontario and is both an amusment park and water park. It is known as one of the best water parks and amusment parks in the world

Castle Fun Park, Originally opened in July 1989 under a different name but rebranded to 'Castle Fun Park' a year later after it's opening. In 2021 the park experienced a massive flood due to a storm and was closed for a over a year but they tried there best to recover and reopened the park as soon as repairs were finished.
