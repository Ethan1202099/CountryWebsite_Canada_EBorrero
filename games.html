Video Games

Assasins Creed, Originally released in 2007. Developed by Ubisoft Montreal in Canada. Over 2.5 million copies sold and a huge franchise still in the gaming industry to this day. Sold over 200 million copies if you combine all the games

Mass Effect, Originally released in 2007. Developed by BioWare which is a studio in Canada. 1.6 million copies Sold within its first six weeks. Has sold over 20 million copies if you combine all the games in the franchise

Cuphead, Originally released in 2017. Developed by independant gaming studio Studio MDHR. Over 6 million copies sold, has won many awards and is known as one of the hardest games of all time
